Privacy Policy

How this site handles your information — kept simple because we keep very little.

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Tim Dolan, a unit owner at 2626 N Lakeview Ave, Chicago, IL 60614, on behalf of the Resident Action Coalition. It exists solely to inform and organize fellow unit owners regarding building governance and special assessments.

Information We Collect

This site is a collection of static HTML pages. We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics platforms, or any third-party services that collect data.

The sign-up form on this site works by opening your default email application with a pre-filled message. When you submit the form, no data is sent to a server or stored in a database. The information you provide is transmitted only through your own email client to the contact address listed on the form.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No analytics or visitor tracking (no Google Analytics, no server logs retained)
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No IP address logging by this site
  • No third-party scripts, widgets, or embedded services

How Your Information Is Used

If you choose to send an email through the sign-up form, the information you provide (name, unit number, professional background, and any other details you include) will be used exclusively for coordinating resident action at 2626 N Lakeview Ave. Specifically:

  • To communicate with you about building governance matters
  • To organize collective owner actions such as board recall petitions or transparency requests
  • To share relevant updates about assessments, meetings, and legal developments

What We Will Never Do

  • Sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party
  • Use your information for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes
  • Publicly disclose your name, unit number, or contact details without your explicit consent
  • Share information with the HOA board, property management, or any opposing party

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic technical information (such as IP addresses) in their standard server logs as part of their hosting infrastructure. This is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not have access to GitHub's server logs.

External Links

This site may contain links to external resources such as Illinois state statutes, legal references, or news articles. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at individuals under the age of 18 and we do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Given the limited scope of this site, substantive changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, contact Tim Dolan at dolan.joseph.timothy@gmail.com.
